My favorite games for each year

2021:

  1. Psychonauts 2: Well worth the wait. I like the first one's atmosphere slightly more, but this one is a lot better mechanically.
  2. There is No Game: Wrong Dimension: A delightful little meta point-and-click "not-a-game".

2022:

  1. Hollow Knight: My favorite game of all time. Everyone should play it at least once.
  2. AI: The Somnium Files: My first venture into Uchikoshi's work, and it did not disappoint.

2023:

  1. Yakuza 0: I really should have played this series sooner. It's a J-drama disguised as a game series and I'm all for it.
  2. Deep Rock Galactic: If you don't Rock and Stone, you ain't coming home. I lost a couple months to this one.

2024 (so far):

  1. Deep Rock Galactic: Survivor: A little barebones as it's still early access, but DRG mechanics work super well with this genre.
  2. Slay the Princess: The sense of humor here is right up my alley. Best played completely blind.
